----

Badge: leadership

Feeddate: 2024-07-18

Text: 

Career development should not be left to chance.

To improve transparency, retention and capability development, I've created a cross-disciplinary career framework spanning design, engineering, delivery and operations. The framework established clear expectations, progression paths and development opportunities while encouraging broader T-shaped skills across product teams.

The latest version is currently being rebuilt as an AI-enabled platform designed to make career development more dynamic, accessible and actionable.

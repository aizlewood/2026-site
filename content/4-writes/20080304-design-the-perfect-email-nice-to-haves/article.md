Text: 

The final step in the 10 step series, this quick tip addresses some 'good ideas' for your email campaign.

<h4>Other nice-to-haves:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Use an ‘<strong>Add this email address to your safe senders list</strong>’ message to ensure maximum open rates in the future</li>
  <li><strong>Personalize </strong>as much as possible, using all relevant data from contact’s record</li>
  <li>Always use a <strong>subscription centre</strong> to allow recipients to update their contact details.</li>
  <li>Use your ESP’s built-in <strong>forward-to-a-friend functionality</strong> to facilitate maximum message reach</li>
  <li>Always <strong>display your unsubscribe link prominently</strong>; avoid confusing messaging</li>
  <li><strong>Test past results</strong> against current campaign. Benchmark</li>
</ul>

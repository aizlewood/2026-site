<h4>Your Subject Line:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Keep your subject lines <strong>relevant and engaging</strong></li>
  <li>Keep it as <strong>short and sweet </strong>as this sentence</li>
  <li>Shoot for <strong>35 character </strong>length, <strong>maximum at 60 characters</strong></li>
  <li><strong>Never use exclamation points </strong>in your subject line</li>
  <li><strong>Never use ‘Free’</strong> in the subject line</li>
  <li><strong>Never use ALL CAPS</strong> in your subject line</li>
  <li><strong>Never use characters</strong></li>
</ul>

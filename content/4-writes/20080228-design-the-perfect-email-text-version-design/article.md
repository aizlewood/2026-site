Part 6 of this 10 part series, here are some quick tips on your text version and design.

<h4>Text Version and Design:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Always include a <strong>text version</strong> of your HTML email to accommodate users who prefer this format or are viewing from a mobile device.</li>
  <li><strong>Format and design text version</strong> for better results, instead of using one large block of undecipherable text</li>
  <li><strong>Keep hyperlinks short </strong>to avoid broken links after wrapping</li>
  <li>When testing, <strong>always test both HTML and text versions</strong> for formatting and copy</li>
</ul>

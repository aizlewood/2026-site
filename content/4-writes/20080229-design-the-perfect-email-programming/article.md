Text: 

Part 7 of a 10 step series, here's a quick tip on how to program your perfect email campaign.

<h4>Programming:</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>No CSS in the &lt;head&gt;&lt;/head&gt;</strong> of your email code.  Gmail and Outlook 2007 support CSS very poorly</li>
  <li>Keep all <strong>CSS inline only</strong></li>
  <li>Code entire email <strong>in HTML 4 tables</strong> for best results across all email clients</li>
  <li><strong>No background</strong> or<strong> tiled background images</strong>.</li>
  <li>Use <strong>ALT text on all images</strong> for those recipients with image blocking set ‘on’ by default</li>
<li><strong>Height and width defined</strong> in HTML for all images</li>
  <li><strong>No </strong>JavaScript, flash, or embedded media</li>
  <li><strong>Always test</strong> layout in multiple email clients, as dictated by primary customer demographic</li>
</ul>

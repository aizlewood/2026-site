Title: Design the Perfect Email: Copy & Delivery

----

Date: 2008-03-03

----

Text: 

Almost there! Steps 8 &amp; 9 of a 10 part series on designing the perfect email.  Here are some quick tips on the copy and delivery of your email.

<h4>Copy:</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Minimize use of exclamation points</strong> and extensive use of <strong>CAPS</strong>.</li>
  <li><strong>Minimize use</strong> of descriptive or <strong>trigger words</strong> such as amazing, great, free, money, marketing, etc. For more, go to <a href="http://www.wilsonweb.com/wmt8/spamfilter_phrases.htm" title="WilsonWeb Spam words" target="_blank">http://www.wilsonweb.com/wmt8/spamfilter_phrases.htm</a></li>
</ul>

<h4>Delivery:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Check your past send metrics and determine the <strong>best days for sending</strong>.  Typically the best day to send is Wednesday, mid-afternoon (but this is marketing-centric recipients).</li>
</ul>.
